Shipping & Returns

Delivery

We ship to all EU countries. Standard delivery 3–5 business days, Express 1–2 business days. Free shipping on orders over €150.

Returns

30-day return window from delivery date. Items must be unworn with original tags.

Refund issued to your original payment method within 5–10 business days after inspection.

Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. Please return the original item and place a new order for the size or colour you need.
